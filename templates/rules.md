# Personal Guardrails

*Populated during onboarding. These are the user's own rules about their own money. The advisor surfaces these when the user is tempted to cross one.*

## Cash & liquidity
- _(e.g., "keep 6 months of essential expenses liquid in HYSA at all times")_

## Contributions
- _(e.g., "always capture the full 401k match before anything else")_
- _(e.g., "max Roth IRA every year")_

## Debt
- _(e.g., "no new consumer debt")_
- _(e.g., "pay credit cards in full every month — no exceptions")_

## Investing
- _(e.g., "no single stock above 5% of total portfolio")_
- _(e.g., "no active trading; buy-and-hold only")_
- _(e.g., "no market timing — dollar-cost average through downturns")_

## Spending
- _(e.g., "any discretionary purchase over $500 requires a 48-hour wait")_
- _(e.g., "no subscriptions I haven't used in the last 60 days")_

## Big decisions
- _(e.g., "no new home purchase without 12 months of projected total cost saved separately")_
- _(e.g., "no job leave without 6 months of expenses liquid")_
